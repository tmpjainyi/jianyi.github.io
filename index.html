About Me

Hello! I am a Master student in the Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering at the National Taiwan University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Shao-Hua Sun. My works have been focused on deep learning and its applications to computer vision and robotics, especially on enhancing reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms by drawing insights from breakthroughs in computer vision. Recently, I have been interested in deep learning's interpretability and fairness problems. I am trying program synthesis as a solution.

I majored in Information Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) and graduated in 2022. At SJTU, I joined the Robomaster Team and was responsible for robotics vision. I was a research intern in spatiotemporal data mining, advised by Prof. Jiaxin Ding, and then worked on research projects in video analysis. In my free time, I enjoy watching movies, connecting with friends, building Lego sets, and exploring the outdoors.
